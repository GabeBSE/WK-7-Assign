What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a free online multiplayer battle arena video game. It was released on October 27, 2009 by Riot Games. It is available to play on Microsoft Windows and macOS.


It has recieved many great reviews from different popular gamming magazines. League of Legend's popularity is big in the gamming community. Riot Games has made it possible to play
internationally. There are cash prize competitions held. You are able to watch competitions on Twitch, Youtube, ESPN.
